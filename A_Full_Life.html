A Full Life

Before your eyes, your life will pass and end before you realize
On a society where legacy is always wanted and sell as the only path in life
Those who struggle, feel like it is pointless to live at all
Because how would you build a legacy?
When you didn't even know how much time left you have

It may be for a terminal illness
Or for a pain, you didn't know how much time more you would be able to withstand
It may be physical or mental, it may be visible or invisible for the other ones
It may be a fear of life ending without being able to do anything at all
Or to ending it with your own hands.

It's scary and frustrating to think
That you are fighting for a future that you may never live to see
But for those struggling with the fear of building a road you will never be able to walk
I want to remind you that a full life does not require a legacy to full be

It doesn't require you to have what society considers a successful life
To be rich, famous or your field revolutionize
Because sometimes we get lost thinking about the life we aim to get
And forget to enjoy the life we are living today

And with that, I'm not saying that your pain is not real or valid at all
I'm saying that thinking the only way to enjoy life is to get to a determined path
Will only make you forget about what life truly are

Because life is not a set-list of objectives to catch
Life is not something that have a goal from a start
The only thing that gives meaning to it
Is the way you impact and connect with others in it

I know you're tired to hear that everything will get better with time
Because time has proven you the contrary many times
So instead, I want you to stop thinking that if you fail your objectives meet
Your life will be pointless and sad

Because even if your life end today
The impact you have on people around you will last
Life is not about what you get
But instead, how you interact

Big and epic stories are overvalued to the point which it is sick how much emphasis society gives us to get one
Because more important of a “legacy” that you would never be able to withstand
Is the impact you have on the people you love and how they will accompany you on your path

Because no amount of magnum opus will be even closer to what
Impact your life will have in the people who around you are
I will not say you to stop thinking about how much time left you have
Because you are the only one who knows the pain you withstand

Rather, I'll tell you that instead of trying to get a legacy in the time you have
Try to enjoy every moment of life you have
That doesn't mean you give up with the future
But to prioritize your present over all

Don't overdo yourself trying to get to a path where you can finally happiness have
Be kind to yourself and do as much for your future as you can without your present ruining for that
If you can't with everything life seems to demand
Let your future plans “fail” for your present to succeed
Because don't let the story of the great life you think you wanted to live
Make you forget about the great life you already had

Even when you feel that only thing in your path was disgrace and dread
If you are still here is because there is something that still make you want to wake up every day
You may not realize it yet
Specially because society has made us think that there has to be something great and meaningful to make us still live

But even if it's your pet or that stream you watch before sleep
If that is what makes you want to live, then let it be
Because the meaning of life should not be determined for anyone but you
Because life is too short to still be wondering if it is valid for something too small
Because at the end, when our life end before your eyes, those small things are what will make you feel like you live a full life
